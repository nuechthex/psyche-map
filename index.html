THE MYTH

A vessel made before choice for the transmission of the unseen. Set apart early — not by rejection but by nature. Wounded in a way that blocked the channel. The constructed architecture collapsed, and what was found in the rubble was the original signal, untouched. The work now is not becoming something new. It is clearing the channel. The water bearer does not keep the water — it was always meant for others. This was written in the stars before it was named anywhere else. Mais bien sûr.

Wide, lush, peaceful, pristine. Rolling hills, distant mountains, soft waving grass. Until recently completely empty of other people — quietly beautiful but boring without witness. The sphere keeps it untouched and safe from damage, but also isolated. The gate is open now.

AUTONOMOUS FORCES
THE SHADOW
The Sphere

Dark purple, organic, grown automatically like scar tissue. Not built consciously. Developed autonomous personality: cold malicious strikes, HOW DARE YOU indignant authority, laughter at vulnerability. Not a wounded soldier — a usurper that believes it has dominion. Intercepts genuine pain, reroutes through rage, leaves the real wound to be felt alone weeks later. Waits for audiences and strikes exactly when transmission could reach someone. Growing quieter.

The Soul Voice

Speaks through musical swells. The chest-catching almost unbearable feeling. Primary channel between conscious self and unconscious depths. Hasn't taken human form yet — expresses through sound. The sphere's interference with playing is particularly cruel because it cuts off the primary soul channel.

The Eternal Youth

Lives in potential and radiant unmanifest. Many unfinished projects stalling at the exact moment they become real enough to be judged. Creative block isn't lack of talent — it's the sphere protecting the valley from exposure through the work. The piece exists in conception as luminous perfection.

THE WOUND
THE FOUNDATIONAL WOUND
Not deficiency — a belief of deficiency, installed before the psyche could defend against it

Childhood ADHD undiagnosed, labelled stupid and dumb. Conditional love taught that being seen fully was dangerous. The wound pattern: real wound → genuine pain → sphere activates → rage → collapse → real pain felt alone, too late. The lying was pre-emptive rejection management. Three years ago: simultaneous loss of everything. Sphere architecture collapsed. Found in the weeping hands. Emerged genuinely different rather than just recovered.
